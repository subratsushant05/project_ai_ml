# Onboarding at Helios Robotics

Welcome to the Helios Robotics engineering team. This handbook section covers
your first two weeks.

## Your first week

Your onboarding buddy will pair with you daily. By the end of week one you
should have your development environment running, access to the internal
package registry, and at least one small pull request merged. Ask your buddy
before filing access tickets; most permissions are granted through the
`eng-access` group.

## Tools we use

We host code on an internal GitLab instance. Continuous integration runs on
shared runners, and every service defines its pipeline in `.gitlab-ci.yml`.
Dashboards live in Grafana and logs are searchable in OpenSearch. Internal
documentation, including this handbook, lives in the `handbook` repository.

## Expectations

During your first month, focus on learning the codebase rather than shipping
large features. Weekly one-on-ones with your manager are the right place to
raise blockers early.
